Professional Summary


A compassionate and dedicated Social Worker with expertise in trauma debriefing, crisis intervention, and psychosocial support. Skilled in providing structured trauma debriefings for individuals and groups, fostering resilience, and supporting recovery following exposure to traumatic events. Our Services

Individual & Group Counselling

Personalized support focusing on trauma recovery, stress, anxiety, and life transitions using evidence-based approaches.

Trauma Debriefing & Crisis Intervention

Structured intervention providing immediate support and psychological first aid following critical events, focusing on stabilization and reducing acute distress.

Critical Incident Stress Management (CISM)

Specialized proactive techniques used shortly after a major incident to reduce stress impact, prevent long-term trauma symptoms, and promote collective recovery in high-risk groups.

Case Management & Risk Assessment

Systematically coordinating essential services and resources to meet client goals, while formally evaluating and mitigating potential risks to the clients or others' safety.

Psychosocial Support & Community Outreach

Holistic support addressing both psychological and social needs, including developing community-based programs to raise awareness and promote mental well-being on a wider scale
