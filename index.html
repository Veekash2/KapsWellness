About Mr. Roshan Singh

Mr. Roshan Singh is a highly experienced social worker with a Bachelor of Arts in Social Work from UNISA. He is dedicated to providing comprehensive wellness and social work services to individuals, families, and communities.

With a compassionate and client-centered approach, Mr. Singh aims to empower clients to overcome challenges, enhance their well-being, and achieve their full potential. His practice is registered with SACS SP (Registration No: 10-25606) and operates under Practice No: 1062980.

KAPS Wellness and Social Work Services is committed to making a positive impact in the lives of those we serve, guided by principles of empathy, respect, and professional integrity.
